ABOUT US

You can experience the American way of life up close at the "YOURS American Sports Bar" in Wiesbaden. The sports bar is located near Wilhelmstrasse, right on the market square, and is easy to reach - whether on foot, by bus, train or car.

We call it a bar, but strictly speaking, it is much more than just that. We cater for any party and thanks to our size, there is room for 10 to 200 guests. Our spacious outdoor dining area also offers space for another 100 guests.

In addition to the great selection of cocktails, the many types of beer, the large number of spirits and non-alcoholic drinks, we offer a fantastic range of American dishes - from homemade hamburgers, spare ribs, chicken wings with fried potatoes and sour cream to muffins and donuts - we have something for every taste.

As a regional sports bar, we offer a lot more than food and drink than meets the eye when you enter the bar: Take a look at a number of famous sports legends that adorn the walls next to the many flat screens! All sports channels and events are shown here around the clock - this is how we make every sporting event an unforgettable group experience. We also regularly present international sporting events live and as everyone knows, a live sporting event is great fun!

Get your own impression of America - food, sports and music. Learn accent-free American for free from the person sitting next to you at your table! Thanks to our numerous American regulars, an international conversation becomes an experience

Our friendly, humorous and well-trained staff provide excellent service.
